Project Overview

This project proposes the design and evaluation of a dedicated Public Safety Broadband Network (PSBN) to support emergency response operations during high-impact events. The objective is to design a resilient, secure, and scalable communication infrastructure capable of maintaining operational integrity when conventional commercial networks experience congestion or failure.

Research Objectives

Reliability

Design a redundant network architecture capable of operating during infrastructure stress.

Security

Implement encrypted communication channels to protect sensitive operational data.

Scalability

Enable deployable mobile base stations to expand coverage during dynamic events.
